Sentence Pattern 06

Subject + Transitive Verb + Direct Object + Adverbial. (S+V+O+A)

  1. They kept water in pitchers.
  2. I will buy a car next week.
  3. I found my lost key in the swimming pool.
  4. The teacher sent the sick child home.
  5. She read the book in the afternoon.
  6. The librarian put the book on the shelf.
  7. She placed the vase on the mantelpiece.
  8. She put all her money in the bank.
  9. He played the guitar last night.
  10. The clerk entered the accounts in the office ledger.
