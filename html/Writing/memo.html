Memorandum

A memoranum or a memo is a type of document used to communicate with others in the same organization. Memos are typically used for fairly short messages of one page or less, but informal reports of several pages also employ memo format.Below are some common types of memos.

  • Policies(changes or new)
  • Instruction
  • Procedures
  • Announcements
  • Trip Reports
