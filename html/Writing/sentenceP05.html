Sentence Pattern 05

Subject + Transitive Verb + Indirect Object + Direct object. (S+V +O(ind)+ O(dir))

  1. He gave me a present.
  2. The postmaster taught Ratan the alphabet.
  3. The lecturer gave all his students A’s.
  4. Grandfather left Ranjith and Dilini all his money.
  5. Dr. Prabath sold me his car.
  6. They sent him a postcard.
  7. He bought his son a bike.
  8. The teacher gave us homework.
  9. We have paid him enough respect.
  10. The old man told us the whole story.
  11. You must tell the police the whole truth.
  12. I have bought my sister a wrist watch.
  13. He didn’t leave us any.
  14. (you) Show me your hands!
