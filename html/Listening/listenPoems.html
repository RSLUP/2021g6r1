Listen to Poems

Good poetry works better through the ears than through the eyes.
Poets develop an ear for the musicality of language, and that's why
we have so many words for the acoustic effects in poetry.
Acoustics refers to the sounds in a poem. Words not only deliver a meaning, but they deliver a sound as well.
